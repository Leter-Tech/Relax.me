# Relax.me

**Escape into Serenity, Find Your Calm 🌿🎵**

Welcome to **Relax.me**, your ultimate destination to unwind, recharge, and find your inner peace. With ambient backgrounds, inspiring quotes, and soothing beats, we bring serenity right to your fingertips.

## Features ✨

### 🌄 **Ambient Backgrounds**
- Immerse yourself in visually stunning landscapes that shift and adapt to your mood.
- Shuffle through an extensive library of calming images for a fresh experience every time.

### 📜 **Inspirational Quotes**
- Discover wisdom and motivation from carefully curated quotes.
- Click "Shuffle Quote" to explore a new perspective whenever you need it.

### 🎵 **Soothing Beats**
- Choose from a variety of sounds, including:
  - 🎹 **Piano melodies**
  - 🌿 **Nature sounds**
  - 💧 **Rain ambience**
  - 🔥 **Crackling fireplace**
  - 📚 **Library vibes**
  - 🌠 **LoFi beats**
  - 🧠 **Binaural tones**
  - ☕ **Cafe ambience**
- Adjust individual sound volumes to create your perfect soundscape.

### ⏲️ **Real-Time Clock**
- Keep track of time with a beautifully styled clock, seamlessly blending function and aesthetics.

### 🖼️ **Customizable Experience**
- Toggle fullscreen for distraction-free relaxation.
- Enjoy seamless transitions between backgrounds and quotes for a fluid, immersive experience.



## How It Works 🚀

1. **Start Relaxing**  
   Open Relax.me and allow the calm to take over. The background and quotes auto-load for instant serenity.

2. **Explore Sounds**  
   Use the "Sound Controls" button to adjust ambient sounds to your liking.

3. **Personalize Your Space**  
   Shuffle backgrounds and quotes with a simple click.

4. **Stay Inspired**  
   Let the curated quotes guide your mind toward positivity and reflection.



## Technology Behind Relax.me 🖥️

- **Frontend:** Developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for a smooth user interface.

- **Firebase Integration:** Real-time database for logging user interactions and sessions.

- **Dynamic Media:** Images and audio dynamically preloaded for a seamless experience.

- **User Authentication:** Powered by Clerk.js for secure and personalized sessions.



## Getting Started 🚀

### Requirements:
- A modern web browser with JavaScript enabled.
- Stable internet connection for loading media and syncing data.

### Steps:
1. Open the app in your browser.
2. Log in or Sign up on account page.
3. Start customizing your relaxation experience!
